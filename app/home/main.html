Push Notifications

Never show up for the non-existent food, yet again.

As an organizer, you can either send out notifications live, or schedule them ahead of time.

Sending notifications live keeps your attendees up to date. When acts are delayed, or venues change, your attendees will know, all without you running around. Notifications can be sent either from our admin panel online, or from our mobile apps.

Scheduling notifications works like a charm. When creating the schedule, you'll be able to choose whether the your attendees should be auto-notified, 5 minutes in advance. You can choose this individually for each event in your schedule, so you don't inundate your attendees with notifications.
